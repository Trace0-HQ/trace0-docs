### Filtering and Searching

You can then filter logs by service and time period.

You can also search logs by free text, returning any log entry where the search term appears in the log entry:

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/logs-search-light.png"
  alt="Logs Light"
/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"
  src="/images/logs-search-dark.png"
  alt="Logs Dark"
/>

Each matching log entry is displayed in the `Log entries` table and includes a link to the corresponding [Transaction](/platform/transactions). Click `View transaction` to open the transaction and see the full context of where the log entry occurred within the transaction.
